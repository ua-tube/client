<h1>Analysis and Development of a YouTube Clone Video Platform</h1>
<p>This diploma project aims to explore and develop a video platform system with functionality similar to YouTube. YouTube stands as one of the most popular video platforms globally, and its success is attributed not only to the quality of its content but also to the efficiency of its platform. Developing a YouTube clone requires thorough analysis of functionality, architecture, as well as considerations for technical aspects, security, and scalability.</p>
